Meet the Founder

Caden Hillier

Founder & CEO

I started building websites when I was 15. Not because anyone told me to, but because I kept seeing great businesses get overlooked online. A restaurant with incredible food and a website that looked like it was made in 2009. A law firm that seemed less trustworthy than a random blog. These were real businesses run by smart people, and they were losing customers before anyone even walked through the door.

That stuck with me. Today, the problem is even bigger. You're not just competing with the business down the street anymore. You're competing with every option a customer can find in three seconds on their phone. Your website is your first handshake, your storefront, and your sales pitch all at once. Most businesses are losing that moment and don't even realize it.

That's why I built Tethron. We do two things really well: we build websites that make businesses look as premium as they actually are, and we install AI employees that handle the stuff that eats up your team's time. Think missed leads getting followed up instantly, customer questions answered at 2am, appointments booking themselves. The kind of work that used to require hiring two more people — except your AI employee works 24/7 and never requires a salary.

Every project is handled by a dedicated team of designers, developers, and automation specialists. I personally oversee every deliverable before it goes out the door. Nothing ships without my sign off. That's how we keep the bar where it needs to be.
