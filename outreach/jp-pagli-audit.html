What Your AI Employee Handles

Five Bottlenecks That Disappear

1

Deal Sourcing & Opportunity Intelligence

Your AI employee monitors private market activity, funding announcements, leadership changes, and thematic signals across your sectors. It flags opportunities that match your thesis before they hit the broader market, compiles a morning deal sheet, and drafts intro notes you can send with a single edit. You wake up to a qualified pipeline, not an empty inbox.

Qualified deal flow delivered daily
2

Due Diligence & Company Research

Feed it a target company, a founder, a sector. Within minutes you have a working diligence brief: financials in public filings, cap table signals, competitive landscape, key personnel backgrounds, recent news, risk flags, and a comparative analysis against your past deals. Every memo written at the level of a senior associate, ready for your review.

Days of research compressed into minutes
3

Investor & LP Communication

Drafts your quarterly LP letters in your voice. Handles one-off investor questions with accurate, on-brand responses routed to you for approval. Tracks who’s engaged with updates, who’s gone quiet, and when to reach out for a check-in. Keeps the relationship layer warm without eating your week.

Every investor feels personally attended to
4

Pitchbook & Materials Engine

Pitchbooks, teasers, one-pagers, and internal memos generated on demand. Your AI employee pulls from your templates, populates with the target’s data, matches your formatting conventions, and has the first draft ready before your next meeting. You edit, refine, and ship. No more late nights rebuilding decks from scratch.

First-draft materials in minutes, not hours
5

Compliance & Regulatory Monitoring

Watches FINRA and SEC updates, regulatory changes, and filing deadlines relevant to your mandates. Flags anything that touches your active deals, books, or investor communications. Prepares routine filings and compliance docs for your review. One less thing to worry about between mandates.

Regulatory changes surfaced before they matter
