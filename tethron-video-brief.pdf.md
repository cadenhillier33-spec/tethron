# Tethron Brand Reel — Creative Brief

## Project Overview
45-60 second brand reel for Tethron (tethron.ai). We install AI employees that run businesses 24/7.

---

## Vibe & Aesthetic
- **Dark premium** (black/near-black backgrounds)
- **Accent color:** teal/cyan (#00d4aa)
- **Clean, cinematic, tech-forward** — think Apple product launch meets fintech
- **Reference site:** tethron.ai (match this exact energy)
- **NO** stock footage of people in offices
- **NO** generic "AI brain" visuals
- This needs to feel like a **luxury tech brand**, not a SaaS explainer

---

- **Logo:** SVG + PNG — double nested triangle, teal gradient
- **Brand colors:** Dark (#070810), Teal (#00d4aa), Secondary Teal (#0A9A87)

## Video Structure

### HOOK (0-4s)
Split into two frames with a **1-second beat** between them:

> **Frame 1:** "Stop managing your business."
>
> *[1 second dark pause — let it land]*
>
> **Frame 2:** "Start owning it."

Dark, dramatic, cinematic pacing.

---

### PROBLEM (4-10s)
Quick cuts, fast pacing, each line hitting harder than the last:

> "Leads dying in your inbox."
> "Customers choosing your competitor."
> "Working weekends just to stay afloat."
> "Hiring people who quit in 6 months."

**Pacing:** FAST and overwhelming. Stacking text, rapid cuts.

---

### REVEAL (10-18s)
**Slow it down.** Let the viewer breathe.

Tethron logo reveal with a premium animation.

> "Meet your AI employee."

---

### WHAT IT DOES (18-35s)
Open with:

> "Right now, someone's AI employee is:"

Then rapid-fire stack — each line appearing on screen and **STAYING**, building into a wall:

> "Answering every customer."
> "Booking every appointment."
> "Running their marketing."
> "Managing their operations."
> "Planning next month's strategy."
> "Collecting revenue they'd never see."
> "Delivering a full business report before sunrise."

All lines visible together, then:

> "All at once. All day. All night."
> "24/7. Dedicated AI employee. Trained on your business."

**Pacing:** Start slow (one line at a time) and ACCELERATE as lines stack. By the end they're appearing rapidly — overwhelming, unstoppable.

---

### TESTIMONIAL (35-39s)
Quote on screen:

> "I haven't touched my phone for work in two weeks."
> — Business owner, Connecticut

---

### THE PUNCH (39-48s)
Side by side or transition:

> "Full-time hire: $4,700 to recruit. $45,000/year."
> "AI employee: $600/month."
> "No salary. No sick days. No turnover."

---

### CLOSE (48-60s)
Tethron logo animation.

> "Your competitors are already running one."
> tethron.ai — **Get Your Free AI Audit**

---

## Overall Pacing Guide

| Section | Feel |
|---------|------|
| Problem | FAST, chaotic, overwhelming |
| Reveal | SLOW, cinematic, breathe |
| What it does | Starts slow, ACCELERATES into a wall of capability |
| Punch + Close | Confident, clean, decisive |
